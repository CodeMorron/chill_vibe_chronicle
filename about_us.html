THE PERSON BEHIND

My Story

Kevin Ruvunangiza is the creative force behind Chill Vibes Chronicles, a platform dedicated to connecting music lovers with the calming world of lofi and chill beats. Growing up surrounded by the vibrancy of different musical genres, Kevin developed an appreciation for music that invites listeners to unwind, focus, and escape into a world of tranquil sounds. With a passion for bringing people together through music, Kevin founded Chill Vibes Chronicles as a place for fans of lofi to discover new artists, explore unique playlists, and immerse themselves in the art of chill vibes. His goal is to build a welcoming community where everyone can find a little peace in the music and inspiration from the stories behind each beat. Through monthly artist features, curated playlists, and insightful content, Chill Vibes Chronicles offers a sanctuary for those seeking a break from the noise and a deeper connection with music. Join Kevin on this journey, and let the soothing sounds of Chill Vibes Chronicles become a part of your daily rhythm.
