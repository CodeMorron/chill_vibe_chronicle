THE PERSON BEHIND

My Story

Welcome to "Chill Vibes Chronicle"! I'm Kevin Ruvunangiza, the founder and curator behind this blog. My love for lofi music started in high school when I discovered its calming beats, perfect for studying and relaxing. With a background in web development and a passion for music, I created "Chill Vibes Chronicle" to share my love for lofi with others. This blog is a space to explore the diverse world of lofi music, featuring artist spotlights, album reviews, curated playlists, and tutorials for aspiring musicians. My goal is to build a community where fans can connect, discover new music, and share their own lofi experiences. When I'm not working on the blog, I'm usually exploring new tunes, working on web design projects, or enjoying a good book with a cup of coffee.
